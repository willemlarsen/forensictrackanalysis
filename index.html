About This Site

This is a resource for Wildlife Trackers, Search and Rescue Trackers, Crime Scene Investigators, and Forensic Scientists. In these pages you'll find tutorials illuminating the topographical patterns generated in a variety of soils by the pressure of the foot.

You'll learn how changes in behavior and movement of the body generate consistent patterns underfoot in the soil.

You'll also discover the physical theory underpinning why substrates deform under pressure in these remarkable patterns.

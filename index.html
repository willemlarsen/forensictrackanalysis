FORENSIC TRACK ANALYSIS

A Depository of Constructal Forms

About This Site

This is a resource for wildlife trackers, Search and Rescue trackers, and forensic scientists. In these pages you'll find tutorials illuminating the patterns generated in a variety of soils by the pressure of the foot and paw.

You'll learn how changes in behavior and movement of the body generate consistent patterns underfoot in the soil.

You'll also discover the physical theory underpinning why substrates deform in these remarkable patterns.
